    AUNSF

    (Anurag University National Student Forum)

    The Anurag University National Student Forum (AUNSF) is an event that brings together bright young minds from all over the country to discuss and find solutions to some of the world’s most critical issues.
    The theme for AUNSF 2023 is “Innovate to Transform”. The theme is aimed at encouraging students to think outside the box and come up with innovative solutions to some of the world’s most pressing global challenges. We aim to ignite a thought of sustainable sensitivity about the community problems and solutions in them.

    DAY-1

    From an inspiring inaugural ceremony to engaging brainstorming sessions, Day 1 was filled with excitement and energy.

    DAY-2

    Day 2 was packed with insightful panel discussions, productive working sessions, and a captivating cultural extravaganza.
    Together, we're making a difference!

    DAY-3

    On the final day, we witnessed remarkable presentations, constructive feedback sessions, and an unforgettable closing ceremony.
